Revenue Leakage Optimization Case Study

Business Problem

Undetected revenue leakage across operational systems resulted in financial inefficiencies and inconsistent forecasting.

Analytical Approach

- Data extraction using SQL
- Data modeling in Python
- Regression-based forecasting
- KPI dashboard creation

Key Insights

Operational discrepancies were traced to underperforming regional processes, revealing leakage concentration in 3 major segments.

Business Impact

18% Revenue Leakage Reduction

25% Operational Efficiency Improvement

3.2x ROI on Analytics Investment
